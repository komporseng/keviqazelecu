What Happens When You Press The Repost Button?

July 30, 2024
blog

Like other social media platforms, TikTok has a repost button that allows users to share videos with their followers. Here's how it works.

TikTok introduced a 'Repost' button back in 2022, but what exactly happens when a user taps on it? The idea of reposting on TikTok is similar to what's offered by other social media platforms. For example, Twitter users can retweet someone else's tweet, while Facebook allows users to share other posts from other accounts to their profile for their friends to see. Even Instagram has a feature that enables users to share posts and Reels to their Stories. Given that most social media apps have a repost or reshare option, it's no surprise that TikTok followed suit.

When users decide to repost a TikTok, the video is likely to appear on their followers' feeds. Only friends of the user can see the reposted video, which is limited to accounts that the user follows that also follow them back. Previously, TikTok only allowed reposts on videos from the For You Page, but now users can repost any video on TikTok. Whether a video is hilarious or has important information, reposting is the best way for users to share it with all their followers.

How To Repost A Video On TikTok

To repost a video on TikTok, open the app and find the video in question. Tap the share (arrow) icon at the bottom right of the screen and select the yellow button with white arrows that says 'Repost.' Tapping this button will repost the video. The repost will not appear on the user's profile, but can appear in their friends' For You Page. Like other social media accounts, the content is credited to the original creator, and is simply being relayed through the user.

After a video has been reposted, the user's profile picture will be displayed above the original creator's name. Users will also have the option to tap on the '+Add comment' button, which will display their comment over the reposted video. When a user adds a comment to a reposted video, it also appears in the comment section of the original video, where other users (and not just the user's followers) can view it.

TikTok also allows users to undo a repost. The method works the same way as reposting, with the user tapping the share button on the video and then 'Remove Repost.' However, there are some caveats. When a user reposts a video on TikTok, it can begin appearing in their followers feeds instantly, so they will need to undo the repost quickly. Additionally, since the reposted video doesn't appear on a user's profile, finding the reposted video in question can be tricky, unless a user knows exactly which account it was from.

Reposting a video on TikTok is simple and effective, and it has already changed how users interact on TikTok. While some users had complained that the repost button had disappeared from their accounts, that was likely due to TikTok still testing the feature. The repost button has since rolled out widely on TikTok, and it looks like the feature is here to stay.
