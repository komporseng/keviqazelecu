Bio:
Glen Powell (born Glen Thomas Powell Jr. on October 21, 1988) is an actor, writer and stuntman who began his career as a child actor in Robert Rodriguez‘s Spy Kids 3D: Game Over. While still acting, he attended the University of Texas where he played competitive lacrosse until he tore his ACL. Since moving to Los Angeles he has appeared in dozens of television shows and movies including NCIS, CSI: Miami and The Expendables 3.

Best Known For:
Glen Powell is best known for starring in Scream Queens with Lea Michele and Emma Roberts. He also had notable roles in Everybody Wants Some!! and Hidden Figures.

Personal Life:
Glen Powell attended the University of Texas at Austin and pledged with Sigma Phi Epsilon fraternity. He has a pet Capuchin monkey named “Charlie”. His romance with Nina Dobrev was confirmed in July 2017 after they were pictured kissing at Julianne Hough’s July 8 wedding.
