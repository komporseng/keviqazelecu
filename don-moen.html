Biography

Don Moen was born in Minneapolis, MN on June 29, 1950. Acclaimed singer and producer of Christian music whose albums God With Us and Mighty Cross both won Dove Awards for Musical Album of the Year. He is the founder of The Don Moen Company and co-host of his own radio show, Don Moen & Friends. He is also a respected songwriter for other artists, having penned songs for singers like Paul Overstreet. He attended the Christian liberal arts school Oral Roberts University and traveled with Terry Law Ministries for ten years.
