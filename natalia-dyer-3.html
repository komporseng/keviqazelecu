Career

Natalia Dyer started her career as an actress in the American film industry and made her debut appearance in the popular Hollywood film titled Hannah Montana The Movie in the year 2009. She played the role of Clarissa Granger and gained worldwide recognition and appreciation for her amazing acting skills.

Since then she made her noted appearances in many popular movies like Too Sunny for Santa, The Greening of Whitney Brown, After Her, After Darkness, and many other popular movies.

In the year 2016, she got a huge breakthrough in her career as she got the opportunity to play the role of Nancy Wheeler in the popular Netflix series titled Stranger Things which gained her huge popularity and fan following.

She has also received the Screen Actors Guild Awards in the year 2017 for her performance in the series Stranger Things, apart from that she has been nominated for several other awards for the same series. In the year 2018, She was starred in the music video titled Wild Love by James Bay.

Awards & Nominations

Natalia Dyer has been nominated for several awards for her role as Nancy Wheeler in the Netflix series “Stranger Things.” She was nominated for Screen Actors Guild Awards for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series in 2017, 2018 and 2019.
