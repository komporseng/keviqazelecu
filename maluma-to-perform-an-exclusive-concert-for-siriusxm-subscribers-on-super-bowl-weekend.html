Maluma To Perform An Exclusive Concert For SiriusXM Subscribers On Super Bowl Weekend

June 29, 2024
blog

Maluma (Teca Lamboglia from São Paulo, Brasil, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons)

LAS VEGAS (CelebrityAccess) — Latin recording artist Maluma will help to kick off Super Bowl weekend with an exclusive concert for SiriusXM and Pandora on Thursday, February 8 at The Theater at Virgin Hotels Las Vegas.

For the show, Maluma will perform cuts from his latest album, Don Juan, including his hits “Según Quién” and “COCO LOCO” along with fan favorites from his extensive catalog of music.

Tickets to the concert will be available to SiriusXM subscribers and Pandora listeners and will be broadcast in its entirety on SiriusXM’s exclusive Latin music channels Hits Uno (ch. 151) and Caliente (ch. 152).

Additionally, music performed in the show will be featured on SiriusXM’s Viva and Flow Nación channels, and listeners will be able to access portions of the show on demand on the new SiriusXM app.

Along with Maluma, the Big Game kick-off concert will feature brand tie-ins with sponsors, including artist meet & greets and a special premium viewing lounge. Sponsors include Chumba Casino, DIscover, Doritos Dinamita, GNC, Norwegian Cruise Line, Paramount+, Shopify, and Hilton.
